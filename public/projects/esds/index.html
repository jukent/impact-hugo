Earth System Data Science

Term: 2020 - 2023
Website: https://ncar.github.io/esds/

NCAR’s Earth System Data Science (ESDS) initiative aims to build a community of technical support for geoscientists by addressing common big data challenges. ESDS has four main goals: to facilitate the development of diagnostic frameworks, to improve coordination on analysis workflows, to explore science at scale, and to develop and curate educational materials.
